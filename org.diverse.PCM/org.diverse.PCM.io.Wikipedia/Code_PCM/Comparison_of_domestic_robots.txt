[[Domestic robot]]s can vary widely in their capabilities and tasks. [[Sensor]]s include: cliff or stair sensors, motion sensors, [[Ultrasonic sensor|ultrasonic object sensors]], dirt sensors, [[Infrared|IR]] sensors, and more. Intelligence varies also. Some have none while others can map out their environment and maneuver using complex [[algorithms]].
