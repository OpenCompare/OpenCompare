CompactFlash SanDisk I 1994 Thinner (3.3 mm), flash only, now up to 512 GB, although standard goes up to 128 PB since CF 5.0

Secure Digital Panasonic, SanDisk, Toshiba, Kodak SDHC 2006 Same build as SD but greater capacity and transfer speed, 4 GB to 32 GB (not compatible with older host devices).
Secure Digital Panasonic, SanDisk, Toshiba, Kodak miniSDHC 2008 62px Same build as miniSD but greater capacity and transfer speed, 4 GB to 32 GB. 8 GB is largest in early-2011 (not compatible with older host devices).
Secure Digital Panasonic, SanDisk, Toshiba, Kodak microSDHC 2007 47px Same build as microSD but greater capacity and transfer speed, 4 GB to 32 GB. (not compatible with older host devices)
Secure Digital Panasonic, SanDisk, Toshiba, Kodak SDXC 2009 100px Same build as SD, but greater capacity and transfer speed, 32 GB and higher. Standard goes up to 2 TB (not compatible with older host devices).
Secure Digital Panasonic, SanDisk, Toshiba, Kodak microSDXC 2009 100px Same build as microSD, but greater capacity and transfer speed, 32 GB and higher. Standard goes up to 2 TB (not compatible with older host devices).
