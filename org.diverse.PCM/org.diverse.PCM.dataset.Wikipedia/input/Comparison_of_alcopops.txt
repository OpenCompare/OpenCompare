'''Alcopop''' (or '''cooler''' in [[Canadian English]] or '''spirit cooler''' in [[South Africa]]) is a colloquial term describing certain flavored [[alcoholic beverage]]s, including:
# [[malt beverage]]s to which various fruit [[juice]]s or other flavorings have been added
# beverages containing wine to which ingredients such as fruit juice or other flavorings have been added ([[wine cooler]]s)
# beverages containing distilled alcohol and added ingredients such as fruit juices or other flavorings
